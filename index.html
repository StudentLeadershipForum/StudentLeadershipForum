about Student Leadership Forum

Student Leadership Forum is a one day program which will bring together different stakeholders ranging from Student Branch Chair / Student Branch Counselor / Student Branch Mentors to Section Student Activities Chair / IEEE Student Activities Volunteers. The forum will be a platform where Student Branch’s are made aware of Life Cycle of IEEE Student Branch which include event & activity reporting, obtaining funding, annual plan etc. The theme of the event is, ’ Culminate. Orient. Look further.’ The program is also intended towards developing the leadership competencies of the students such as career planning and the role of an active community like IEEE Young Professionals as a part of their journey.

What is Student Leadership Forum?

Student Leadership Forum is a platform for the student members from different Student Branches to culminate together, orient themselves towards the opportunities available for the IEEE student members as look further after graduation as theYoung Professionals.

What will happen in the Student Leadership Forum?

What can we experience by attending this event ?

We plan to provide an exciting one day experience for the students with,
1. Training sessions from the IEEE leaders
2. Hands-on workshops on leadership and career development
3. Leadership Forum with the outstanding volunteers and experts in the technical field
